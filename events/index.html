Events

Tutorial nights and other activities.

What events does CIGMAH run?

We try to run tutorial nights throughout the year on a range of topics which relate to coding and medicine. These tutorial nights are usually aimed at beginners, so no prior coding experience is necessary.

We also try to run a Puzzle Hunt sometime during the year, but this depends on our available resources.

This section will be updated throughout the year.
